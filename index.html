About Me

Tinkerer and Researcher

I've always loved solving puzzles: breaking down large problems and processes into smaller, more manageable pieces, and then following back to how they fit together. It's what drew me to software engineering, and once I'd done my first coding exercise, I was hooked!

Accountant turned Coder

My background in accounting has helped hone my pattern recognition, so I'm always up for digging through documentation, checking old issues, asking new questions, and documenting the problem-solving process for future reference.

Debugging & QA Fan

Having worked in e-commerce inventory auditing and control, I'm very used to hearing, "Something went wrong. We don't know what, but the numbers are off. Fix it!" Figuring out how a process is meant to work, what didn't go right, and how to prevent the error from happening again is my kind of challenge!

Full Stack Developer

I compare full stack development to being bilingual. My knowledge of both English and Russian enhances my understanding and proficiency in each of them, giving me insights I wouldn't have otherwise gained. Expanding into back end development has likewise given me a better grasp of front end processes and practices, and vice versa.
